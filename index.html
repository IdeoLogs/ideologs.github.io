What is Essence Sort?

Essence Sort is a test designed for the world of Naalsem, to see what essence type you might embody. Don't take it too seriously, it's more like a game.

This test uses the SapplyValues UI There are questions in the test.

What do my results mean?

In the world of Naalsem, there are four essential types which everything is made of. Red is the body, blue is the mind, yellow is the soul, and purple is the will. Each person has all four essence types, but depending on your individual traits you have an "essentially affinity," which is the type you embody most.
